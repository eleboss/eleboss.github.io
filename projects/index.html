Efficient Spatial-Temporal Normalization of SAE Representation for Event Camera

S. Lin, F. Xu, X. Wang, W. Yang, L. Yu.

In this work, we propose a highly efficient normalization method named chain normalization and an improved ordering strategy. By embedding the inherent nature of SAE, our method can not only runs fast but also reach the highest performance without manual parameter tuning. In the experiment, our algorithm can run significantly faster than the previous methods. To further validate the normalized results, we conduct object recognition experiments on two large event-based open datasets. Experimental results show that our method achieves the highest classification accuracy among other normalization methods.

Toward Autonomous Rotation-Aware Unmanned Aerial Grasping

S. Lin, J. Wang, R. Peng, W. Yang, G. Xia.

In this project, a vision-based autonomous UAM with a 3DoF robotic arm for rotational grasping and with a displacement compensation system for center of gravity compensation are proposed. And, a novel detection approach called Rotation-SqueezeDet is proposed to enable rotation-aware grasping, this approach can gives the target position and rotation angle in near real-time on Jetson TX2.

Tech: ROS C++ Python Tensorflow Solidworks Latex
